Last Meal

Description:
Last Meal is a chef-themed game where the player hunts monsters at night and runs a restaurant by day. The player will battle monsters in a Vampire Survivor–style arena, collecting their parts as ingredients for new dishes.

Afterward, the player returns to the kitchen to cook meals, serve customers, and earn coins to upgrade their weapons for the next night.
